Welcome to Open Sudoku Homepage.

Open Sudoku is a simple open source sudoku game. It's designed to be controlled both by finger and keyboard. It's preloaded with 90 puzzles in 3 difficulty levels, more puzzles can be downloaded from the web and it also allows you to enter your own puzzles.

You can install Open Sudoku via Google Play Store.

About Puzzles

Open Sudoku can open sudoku files directly from web. Just click any link below and folder with puzzles will be downloaded and imported directly into the Open Sudoku.

  • Medium (100 puzzles)

  • Very Hard (100 puzzles)
